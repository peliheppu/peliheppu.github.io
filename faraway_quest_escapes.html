Faraway Quest - Escapes

Faraway Quest - Escapes is an escape room game. It is specifically designed for players who crave a true mental workout.

Step into an exciting escape room adventure and solve mysteries in six uniquely themed rooms filled with puzzles. Discover clues, unlock secrets, and unravel the enigmas to successfully navigate your way to freedom.

Rooms

Temple Mystery

In this game, you find yourself in an ancient temple surrounded by symbols whose meaning was lost long ago. Solve the mystery of the temple but escape before meeting the same fate as those who came before you.

Phantom Of The Theatre

Escape a haunted theatre backroom. The entire theatre group is on tour, and the phantom of the theatre is sad and lonely. It's holding you there, as it has no one else. You need to figure out a way to escape.

Scientist's Laboratory

Escape the laboratory of a scatterbrained and paranoid scientist, who has hidden the key. Fortunately he has left some clues. Decipher his hidden clues and puzzles to find the key.

The Spy Who Disappeared

This game takes you into the authentic 1980s spy world. Agent 85 has gone missing. Your mission is to determine Agent 85's whereabouts using the clues found in the agent's apartment.

Vampire Crypt

Welcome to the vampire's crypt, a place steeped in mystery and secrets. Finding the key is your best chance for a safe escape.

Old Lighthouse

You were shipwrecked on a deserted island with a darkened lighthouse. Try to switch on the lighthouse lantern and get help before the smugglers seizing the island return.
